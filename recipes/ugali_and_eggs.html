Scrambled Eggs :)

        This a common mean in the heart of kenya and yes for you to be full, Ugali must be there.
        This is meal that atleast 90% of the kenyan household have prepared within their lifetime.
        So tag along for you to know how to make one for yourself.
        Karibu Sana!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 eggs
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2 tomatoes cubed
  • 1 small bell pepper/ hoho optional
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger ground
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Chillies optional
  • 1 bunch Dhania/coriander
  • 2 tablespoons Oil

Preparation steps

  1. On a heated pan, fry the onions in the vegetable oil until the onions are soft and slightly brown.
  2. Add in the ground ginger and garlic and fry until fragrant.
  3. Add in the curry powder and cook for a further minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes and bell pepper, if using, and cover on low heat.
  5. Uncover once the tomatoes are cooked and soft.
  6. Add about 1/4 cup of water and allow the tomatoes to cook further into a sauce.
  7. Next up, add in the eggs, one by one and season with some salt.
  8. Stir the eggs slowly and consistently as the eggs continue to solidify.
  9. Keep mixing and scrambling until all the eggs are dry and cooked through.
  10. Finish off with the chilies, if using, and the dhania.
  11. Serve hot with Ugali or freshly toasted bread!
